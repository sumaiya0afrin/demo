Introduction

There are numerous natural wonders for which we should be grateful. Flowers are undoubtedly one of them. Flowers come in a variety of shapes and sizes, and we see them all around us. The wonderful scents and flowers add to the scenic scenery of our planet. We shall look at what these lovely objects do and how much delight they bring.


Importance of flower

Flowers hold a significant place in our lives.Flowers are also used for important occasions such as weddings.To symbolize their marriage, the bride and groom wear flower garlands. Furthermore, flowers have such a pleasant scent that we use them in a variety of ways, including planting them in our yard. Our environment's attractiveness is enhanced in this way.Flowers are important in every corner of the world. They are also used to make medicines. The scent of perfume is different from that of flowers. In addition, butterflies, birds and bees eat flowers as food.
